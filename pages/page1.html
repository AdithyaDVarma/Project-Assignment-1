Description

Superman is one of the most powerful superheroes in the DC Universe. As a symbol of hope, he stands for truth, justice, and the American way. His character represents the ideal of selflessness and heroism, often facing formidable foes such as Lex Luthor and Doomsday. He inspires many through his actions and beliefs, making him a timeless figure in comic book history.
