Iron Man

Origin

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor. After being kidnapped by terrorists, he built a suit of armor to escape and decided to use his technology to protect the world. With his intellect and resources, Tony Stark becomes Iron Man, fighting against threats to humanity.

Powers

  • Genius-level intellect
  • Expert engineer and inventor
  • Powered armor suit with superhuman strength
  • Flight capabilities
  • Advanced weaponry (repulsor beams, missiles)
  • Artificial intelligence assistance (J.A.R.V.I.S. / F.R.I.D.A.Y.)

Description

Iron Man is a key member of the Avengers and a symbol of technological advancement in the Marvel Universe. His character represents the duality of human flaws and heroic ideals, often dealing with personal demons while striving to be a force for good. Stark's charisma, humor, and determination make him one of Marvel's most iconic heroes.
