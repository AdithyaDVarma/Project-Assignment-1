Thor

Origin

Thor, the God of Thunder, is a member of the Asgardian royal family and the son of Odin. He wields the enchanted hammer Mjölnir, which grants him the ability to control lightning and fly. After being cast down to Earth, he learns humility and becomes a protector of both Asgard and humanity.

Powers

  • Superhuman strength and durability
  • Control over thunder and lightning
  • Ability to fly using Mjölnir
  • Expert warrior and combat strategist
  • Longevity and resistance to aging

Description

Thor is a powerful superhero in the Marvel Universe, balancing his responsibilities as a god and a hero. His character embodies bravery, honor, and a deep sense of duty. Thor's adventures often involve epic battles and cosmic threats, showcasing his growth and understanding of what it means to be a true hero.
