Hulk

Origin

The Hulk, also known as Bruce Banner, is a brilliant scientist who was exposed to gamma radiation during an experiment gone wrong. This exposure transformed him into the Hulk, a creature of immense strength and rage. Banner struggles to control his alter ego while using his scientific mind to find solutions to various challenges.

Powers

  • Superhuman strength (increased with anger)
  • Enhanced durability and healing factor
  • Ability to leap great distances
  • Gamma radiation immunity
  • Exceptional intellect in science and engineering

Description

The Hulk represents the struggle between intellect and emotion. As a member of the Avengers, he fights for justice while battling the personal conflict of his dual nature. His raw power and capacity for destruction are tempered by Banner's desire to do good, making him one of Marvel's most complex characters.
